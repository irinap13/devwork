iPad application design for events

At Concise Media Design, I work both as a designer and front-end developer. As a designer, my tasks are creating mockups for iPad applications for various clients and the events they hold worldwide. The Interactive Conferences London application is a demo one, which Concise uses to show prospective clients the functionality of their applications, among other services they provide.

The iPad applications have multiple modules which help the event organisers interact with their delegates. Designs, modules and the application content are personalised for every client and their corporate identity.

The Concise iPad applications also come ready for portrait orientation.
